About the Project

Light of Quran is an open-source automation project dedicated to spreading the message of the Holy Quran through modern digital media. By leveraging the Meta Graph API and high-quality rendering engines, we create engaging content that brings the wisdom of the Quran to the palms of millions.

Our mission is to make Quranic knowledge accessible, beautiful, and consistent in the fast-paced world of social media.

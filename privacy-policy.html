Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect it.

Effective: May 14, 2026 Updated: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Light of Quran ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the "Light of Knowledge" application and associated services that publish Quranic content across social media platforms including Instagram, Facebook, and Threads (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our Service, including our "Daily Quran Verses" Facebook Page and associated Instagram and Threads profiles.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected (Aggregated Only)

When you interact with our social media content (liking, commenting, sharing, or viewing our Reels and Carousels), the respective platform may provide us with aggregated analytics data:

  • Post engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, views)
  • Aggregate audience demographics (age ranges, regions)
  • Content performance insights

This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Individual user tracking across websites or platforms
  • User login credentials or Facebook Login data
  • Payment or financial information

3. How We Use Information

The limited, aggregated data we receive is used solely to:

  • Improve content: Understanding which Quranic content resonates most.
  • Optimize scheduling: Determining the best publishing times for reach.
  • Monitor performance: Ensuring our automated system works correctly.

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

4. Meta Platform Data

Our application uses the Meta Graph API to publish content. We use these permissions:

API Permissions Used

  • pages_manage_posts: Publish Reels and carousels to our Facebook Page.
  • instagram_content_publish: Publish Reels and carousels to our Instagram.
  • pages_read_engagement: Read engagement metrics on our content.
  • read_insights: Access page-level analytics.

We only access and manage our own Page and Instagram account. We do not access any end-user's personal accounts, messages, photos, or private data.

Our use complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or transfer your information. Third-party services involved:

  • Meta Platforms: Content hosted on Meta's platforms, subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud (Gemini AI): Generates captions from Quranic text only — no user data is sent.
  • GitHub: Hosts source code and media. No user data stored.

6. Data Retention & Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data retention. We implement industry-standard security: encrypted API tokens, environment variable storage, regular token rotation, HTTPS for all communications, and restricted administrator access.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights including access, deletion, and objection depending on your jurisdiction. Since we hold no personal data, requests will receive a "no data held" confirmation. See our Data Deletion page for deletion instructions.

For EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

We acknowledge all GDPR data subject rights. Since no personal data is collected, requests will be confirmed with a "no data held" response.

8. Cookies, Children & Changes

Cookies: This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Social media platforms have their own cookie policies.

Children: Our content is educational and suitable for all ages, but our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes: We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes.
